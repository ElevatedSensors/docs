# Getting Started

Welcome to the Bed Presence MK1 Getting Started guide. This page will walk you through everything you need to get your
sensor up and running, from connecting it to your WiFi and smart home platform to installing it on your bed. Whether
you’re a first-time user or an advanced hobbyist, this guide provides step-by-step instructions, helpful tips, and links
to additional resources.

By the end of this guide, you’ll have your Bed Presence MK1 fully provisioned, integrated with your home automation
system, and calibrated for accurate detection.

## What You'll Need
- Bed Presence Mk1
- Universal Mounting Kit (if needed)
- USB power supply and USB-C cable
    - 5V @ 500mA
- 2.4Ghz WiFi
